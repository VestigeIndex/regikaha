# Product Spec

## Audience

Self-employed tradespeople, contractors and small construction companies operating in Regi Kaha's selected European markets.

## Core journeys

1. Capture a lead from Regi Kaha or manually and convert it into a client.
2. Create a job linked to a client, track status, dates, budget and up to five compressed photos.
3. Build a line-item quote with discount and country-appropriate configurable VAT/tax.
4. Record an internal acceptance signature and export a PDF with a verification QR.
5. Normalise a material description and compare transparent demonstration options.
6. Find a supplier, open device-native directions and call directly.
7. Manage team members, company identity, currency, tax and product plan.

## Trust rules

- Demonstration prices are labelled and are never presented as live supplier offers.
- A drawn signature is labelled as internal acceptance, not a qualified electronic signature.
- Verification codes establish local document identity; they are not described as blockchain or third-party certification.
- Local persistence is not described as cloud synchronisation.

## Accessibility and mobile

Controls target at least 40-44 px, forms have labels, icon buttons have accessible names, tables become readable records on narrow screens, and Arabic switches the document to RTL.
